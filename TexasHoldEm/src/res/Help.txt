HELP/TERMINOLOGY:
Check(Call): Match the previous bet
Bet/Raise: Money wagered during the play of a hand
Fold: Throw away your hand and show disinterest in current pot
Blind: Forced bet (small blind is smaller of the two forced bets)
Community cards: Shared cards placed faced up at the center of the table

OBJECTIVE: Put pressure on your opponent and build pots

STRATEGY: 
Judge your opponent.
Play aggressively aggainst weaker opponents.
Play defensively against stronger and more aggressive opponents.
